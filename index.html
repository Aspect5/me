Things I keep coming back to. Not finished thoughts, just directions.

Learning & AI

  • The substitution problem — Students use AI to skip thinking. The tool that promised to democratize learning is making them worse. How do you build something that resists this?
  • Knowledge as a graph — Understanding isn't a score. It's a structure: prerequisites, dependencies, gaps. How can you map and use it?

Systems

  • Agentorchestration — AIs, like humans, work better in teams. And like human organizations, their structures can vary widely. What are the best ways to coordinate them?
  • Cybernetic governance — Control theory, AI, and systems science can help us govern complex societies and organizations through feedback loops. How can we do this in a way that puts humans first?
